Usage:

hashtag [argument] <name>

  <name>  Use alias.
  -h  Show this help message.
  -s  Subscribe to a hashtag.
  -u  Unsubscribe from a hashtag.
  -g  Get hashtags for the image.

  -l  [hashtag]
If hashtag is specified, shows all users subscribed to it. If not, then shows hashtags that you are subscribed to.
